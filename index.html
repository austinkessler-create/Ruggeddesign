Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a deck build take? ↓
Most custom deck builds take 1–2 weeks once permits are in hand. Permitting and scheduling typically adds 2–4 weeks before work begins. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
What's the difference between composite and wood decking? ↓
Composite decking (like Trex or TimberTech) requires very little maintenance and resists rot, fading, and splintering. Wood offers a classic look and lower upfront cost but needs regular upkeep. We'll help you choose what's right for your home.
Do you handle permits? ↓
Yes — we handle all permitting as part of our process. You don't need to navigate city requirements on your own. We take care of it from start to finish.
How soon can I get an estimate? ↓
We typically schedule a site visit within a few days of your inquiry and deliver a written estimate within 24 hours of our visit. Fill out the contact form and we'll be in touch quickly.
